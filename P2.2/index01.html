RED AND HER FRIENDS

Devon “Red” Briggs, owner and red head girl boss of Red and Friends opened the store in 2020 next to Portlands long time favorite, Bingo Used Books. The 2,000-square-foot space is home to many vendors who overlap with other vintage stores in Portland, and a few single booth owners.
